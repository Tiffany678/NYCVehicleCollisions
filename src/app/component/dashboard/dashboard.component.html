There are about 1000,000 car accidents in New York every year. Every month, the NYPD publishes crash data for all five city boroughs. This is the simple dashboard showing the analyzed results from 12/01/2022 to 12/04/2022. And due to the limitation of the NYC Open Data API, only the result of analzyhing 1,000 records is shown below.
